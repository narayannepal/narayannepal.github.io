About Me

I am Narayan Nepal.
Concept of my birth that begins from Bhojpur gets its form at Chatara in Sunsari.
I grow up mostly at my own place of birth where I get a chance to make nominal trips to bordering parts (up to Khasa) in China, south-west parts of Bhutan, and some of the parts in India.I remain keen to come and in-law member of Morang (Madhumara-Biratnagar) where my spouse originally comes from. I take to higher studies at Dharan, at Birganj and Kathmandu itself.
I am interested in using and promoting oral narrative poetry ( सबाई / Sabaai), classical metrical poetry (chhanda kavita / Sanskrit-Nepali), haiku, muktak, ghazal, and other forms of poetry and prose.
My interest portfolio also extends to karmakanda, judiciary, and English language teaching.
I love to hum some of the varieties of line notes of musically seasoned weight and particularly of my own lyrics (swadesh gaan, pop/rap songs, modern songs, ghazals, bhajan, and folk songs) the most, and to enjoy cyber linkage.
I am akin to my faith which I believe is not to be the mark of trade on any names, or by any means. I am proud to be a Nepalese (Nepali) first, and a number of other words of identity are naturally my emblems thereon.
My books (both in Nepali and in English), and my work relating to some other socio-academic disciplines will bespeak more about me, and my being of my self is on.
